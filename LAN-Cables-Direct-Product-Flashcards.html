What is a Fiber Optic Trunk Cable, and what job does it do in the data center?
A Fiber Trunk Cable (also called a backbone cable) is a multi-fiber pre-terminated cable that runs between patch panels, spine switches, or distribution frames. It carries many fiber channels in a single jacketed bundle, forming the high-capacity backbone of the data center's fiber plant.
